THE OWNERSHIP SCRIPT

Team & Leadership

WHEN YOUR TEAM MISSES TARGET

"We missed the mark this week — and that starts with me. I didn't set us up with enough leads, or I didn't coach you hard enough on conversions. So here's what I'm changing: [specific action]. Now I need each of you to name one thing you'll own differently next week."

WHEN YOU NEED TO DELIVER TOUGH NEWS

"I have some tough news to share, and I want to be direct because you deserve that. [Deliver news.] I know this isn't easy to hear. I want you to know I'm taking responsibility for how we got here, and here's exactly what I'm doing about it: [action]."

FAITH IN LEADERSHIP: Leading with accountability doesn't mean carrying the burden alone. It means modeling the posture you want your team to adopt. When you say "that's on me," you give your team permission to own their part too — without shame.
