PILLAR 2

Words Create Worlds

Your language either opens doors or closes them. Choose deliberately.

Instead of...

Say this...

"The market is tough right now."

"I'm refining my approach for this market."

"My client didn't show up."

"I didn't confirm the meeting powerfully enough."

"I can't afford that."

"I'm choosing where to invest right now."

"That's just how it is."

"That's where it is — and here's my next move."

"I have to..."

"I choose to..."

ACTION:

Pick your most common excuse. Rewrite it using the ownership frame above. Use it for the next 7 days.
