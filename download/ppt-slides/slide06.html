THE OWNERSHIP SCRIPT

When a Client Hesitates

WHEN A CLIENT HESITATES

"I appreciate you sharing that concern. Let me be honest — I should have addressed this earlier in our conversation. Here's what I want you to know: I take full responsibility for making sure you feel confident. So let me ask — what would need to be true for you to feel 100% about moving forward?"

WHEN A PROSPECT GHOSTS

"Hey [Name], I haven't heard back and I want to own my side of this — I clearly didn't make the value compelling enough, or I missed something that matters to you. Either way, that's on me. If you're still open, I'd love one more chance to get it right. If not, I completely understand."

PRO TIP:

The magic isn't in the script — it's in the willingness to say "that's on me." Ownership disarms resistance faster than any closing technique.
