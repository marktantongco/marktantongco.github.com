RITUAL

The Weekly Reset

15 minutes every Friday at 4:30 PM — no excuses, no skipping.

1

REVIEW

What were my commitments this week? Did I honor them? Write down every promise you made — kept or broken.

2

REFLECT

Where did I blame instead of own? Name one moment you pointed outward. Rewrite it with the ownership frame.

3

RECHARGE

What fills my cup? Guard your energy. List one thing that restored your spirit this week — do more of it.

4

PLAN

What are my top 3 commitments for next week? Write them. Share them. Own them before Monday arrives.

Schedule it now: Friday 4:30 PM — Review, Reflect, Recharge, Plan. 15 minutes that change everything.
