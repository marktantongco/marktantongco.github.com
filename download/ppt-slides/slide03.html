PILLAR 1

Fix the Problem, Not the Blame

"When something goes wrong, ask not 'Whose fault?' — ask 'What do I own, and what's my next move?'"

1

Identify What You Own

Before pointing outward, scan inward. Ask: "What part of this outcome did I create, allow, or fail to prevent?"

2

Name the Fix, Not the Fault

Replace "They didn't follow up" with "I didn't create a follow-up system." Language rewires ownership.

3

Take One Action Within 24 Hours

Accountability without action is just guilt. Commit to one concrete move before the sun sets tomorrow.
