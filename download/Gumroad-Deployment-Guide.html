Phase 4

Set Pricing & Variants

Step 10
Set the Base Price
  1. In the product editor, go to Settings > Pricing
  2. Set Price: $27 USD
  3. Enable "Pay What You Want" with minimum $27 (allows generous buyers to pay more — common in faith-driven communities)
Step 11
Create the Team License Variant

Use Gumroad's Variant feature to offer a team license upsell:

  1. Go to Settings > Variants
  2. Create Variant Group: "License Type"
  3. Variant 1: "Individual License" — $27
  4. Variant 2: "Team License (up to 5 members)" — $97

The Team License includes everything in the Individual License plus permission to share with up to 5 team members. This is where most of your revenue will come from — agency directors buy for their entire team.

Pricing Psychology: At $27, this is an impulse buy for an MDRT-earning advisor. The $97 team license feels like a steal for 5 people ($19.40 each). Most agency directors won't blink at $97 because they spend more than that on a single team lunch. Position it as an investment, not an expense.
Step 12
Enable Affiliate Program
  1. Go to Settings > Affiliates
  2. Enable the affiliate program
  3. Set commission rate: 30%
  4. This means affiliates earn $8.10 per individual sale, $29.10 per team sale
Affiliate Strategy: Don't just enable affiliates — actively recruit 3 trusted MDRT peers. Send them the playbook for free first, then invite them to become affiliates. When they experience the value personally, their promotions become authentic, not salesy. Your network is your distribution channel with zero ad spend.

Step 19
Soft Launch — Get Your First 5 Testimonials

Before promoting publicly, share the link with 5 trusted peers for initial feedback and testimonials:

  1. Send a personal message (not a mass email): "Hey [Name], I just launched my playbook. I'd love your honest feedback — and if it helps you, a review on Gumroad would mean the world."
  2. Give them free access if needed (Gumroad lets you generate free download links)
  3. Collect 3-5 reviews before your public launch
  4. Reviews are the #1 conversion driver on Gumroad — don't skip this
Launch Sequence: Don't announce to everyone at once. Start with your 5 closest peers (Day 1), then your MDRT circle (Day 2-3), then your full network (Day 4-7). Each wave builds social proof for the next. When your full network sees the product already has 5 positive reviews, conversion rates jump significantly.

The Typeform link inside your PDF (https://marktantongco.typeform.com/to/playbook-takeaway) serves three strategic purposes:

Set a monthly reminder to review Typeform responses and incorporate them into quarterly updates.
