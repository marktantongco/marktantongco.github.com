# Client Follow-Up System
*Copy this into Notion or Google Docs*

---

## The 3-Touch Rule

Never let a warm lead go cold. Use this sequence:

### Touch 1 (Within 24h of meeting)

"Great connecting today, [Name]. As promised, here's [resource]. One question I'm still thinking about from our chat: [personalized question]. No need to reply now—just wanted to share."

### Touch 2 (Day 4)

"Hi [Name], circling back on [specific topic]. I found this [article/case study] that relates to what you mentioned about [their goal]. Thought you might find it useful."

### Touch 3 (Day 7)

"Hi [Name], last nudge from me this week. If now's not the right time to explore [solution], no worries at all. If you're still curious, I have 2 slots open next week for a quick 15-min chat. Either way, wishing you a great week."

---

## Pipeline Tags (Use in CRM)

- **BLUE - Warm**: Met, needs nurturing
- **YELLOW - Hot**: Ready to decide, follow up in 48h
- **GREEN - Closed**: Client onboarded
- **WHITE - Cold**: Not now, revisit in 90 days

---

## Weekly Review Prompt

Every Monday AM:

1. Filter for **YELLOW - Hot** leads
2. Send Touch 2 or 3 as needed
3. Move stale leads (>14 days no reply) to **WHITE - Cold** + schedule 90-day reminder

---

## The Faith Angle

Before each follow-up, pause for 10 seconds: "Lord, help me serve this person, not sell to them." This simple prayer shifts your energy from pressure to purpose—and clients can feel the difference.
