# 15-Minute Team Huddle Agenda
*Run this every Monday 9 AM*

---

## Timebox Strictly

- **0:00-2:00** → Wins & Gratitude (each person shares 1 win + 1 thanks)
- **2:00-8:00** → Blockers & Solutions (What's stuck? What do you need from me?)
- **8:00-12:00** → Priority Focus (Each person states TOP 1 goal for the week)
- **12:00-15:00** → Commitment Close ("I commit to ______ by Friday")

---

## Rules

- No problem-dumping without a proposed solution
- No blaming clients, market, or tools
- If it takes >2 mins, park it for 1:1 later
- Every complaint must include an ownership reframe

---

## Leader Prep (Do This Sunday PM)

1. Review last week's commitments
2. Prepare 1 coaching insight based on team patterns
3. Bring coffee or snacks (small gesture, big morale boost)
4. Pray for your team before the huddle—ask for wisdom to lead well

---

## Sample Commitment Close

Each person says:

> "I commit to [specific action] by [specific time] on Friday."

The leader records each commitment and follows up on Friday afternoon.

---

## Weekly Accountability Check

After the huddle, log each team member's commitment in your Accountability Tracker. On Friday, review: Did they hit it? If not, use the Ownership Script: "What part of the process felt unclear? What support do you need from me?"
